Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This policy explains how Pixel Crime City handles data when you use the website and game at pixelcrimecity.com.

Information We Store

  • Player profile details you enter in-game (for example: display name, color, profile ID).
  • Gameplay progression and stats needed for quests, reputation, and leaderboards.
  • Server logs required for stability, debugging, and abuse prevention.

Cookies and Local Storage

The game stores settings and profile information in browser local storage. Admin authentication uses secure session cookies. We may also use consent and ad-related storage where legally required.

Ads and Consent

We may display third-party ads. Where required by law, ad personalization and certain storage are shown only after user consent through a compatible consent mechanism.

How We Use Data

  • Operate multiplayer sessions, persistence, and live gameplay systems.
  • Display leaderboards and anti-abuse protections.
  • Measure service reliability and improve gameplay quality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited technical data with infrastructure and advertising providers strictly to run the service.

Your Choices

  • You can clear browser storage to remove locally stored profile/settings.
  • You can control ad-related consent through consent prompts where available.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use the contact details on Contact.
